Catalog of Bread and Cake

This comforting cake is filled with grated carrots, walnuts, and raisins. The warm spices and sweet flavors make it a perfect treat for fall. The cake is topped with a creamy cream cheese frosting and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

This classic cake is a beautiful red hue with a moist, velvety texture. The cake is frosted with a creamy cream cheese frosting and decorated with a sprinkle of edible glitter. The combination of flavors is sweet and tangy, making it a perfect dessert for any occasion.

This refreshing cake is perfect for a summer celebration. Layers of moist sponge cake are infused with pineapple and coconut, giving it a tropical flavor. The cake is frosted with a tangy passion fruit glaze and garnished with fresh mango slices.

This delicate cake is light and airy, with a soft, fluffy texture. The white chiffon cake is topped with a cloud-like meringue frosting and a scattering of fresh raspberries. The combination of flavors is refreshing and delightful.

This towering cake is a chocolate lover's dream. Layers of moist chocolate sponge cake are sandwiched together with a rich chocolate frosting. A drizzle of dark chocolate ganache adds a final touch of decadence. The cake is topped with a dusting of cocoa powder and a few chocolate shavings for a truly indulgent treat.

This rustic lemon cake is a burst of citrusy flavor. The moist sponge cake is infused with lemon zest and juice, giving it a tangy and sweet taste. The cake is topped with a lemon glaze and a sprinkle of fresh lemon zest for an extra zesty kick.

Authers: Jacob Leary and Aaryamann Dev Sharma
